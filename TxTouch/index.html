About Touch Rugby

Touch is a sport that can be played in all weather, including snow

Rules of the Game

Touch Rugby is a game that involves two teams of 6 playing against each other through 6 touches. Once the team with the ball has been touched six times, possession changes over. Unlike other games of rugbby, there is no kicking in touch.
